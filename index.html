Bio

My primary research interests lie in the field of artificial intelligence, particularly but not limited to representation learning and computer vision. I am a fourth-year student majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering with a minor in Astronomy at Yonsei University. I believe that the ultimate goal of artificial intelligence is to create systems that are logically interpretable, explainable, and reliable. In my most recent work, I have conducted research on causal disentanglement in images. Currently, I am engaged in research at Linq Labs, focusing on the control of Large Language Models (LLM) through in-context learning. Given the significance of logic in certain domains, I am also considering Neuro-Symbolic AI as my potential research topic.
